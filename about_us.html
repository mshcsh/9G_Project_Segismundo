What is this website for?


In the Philippines, March is designated as Fire Prevention Month, making it the ideal time to talk about and spread knowledge of the causes, risks, and dangers of fire. The Philippines has been frequent with fire accidents. This is why we established this website. A fire prevention strategy can assist prevent a fire from starting in the first place and considerably limit the amount of damage that a fire may do. A fire emergency evacuation plan outlines how you and your personnel can leave the building in a safe and suitable way from any location.

This website will provide important tips to prevent fire in homes, workplaces, schools, etc.
